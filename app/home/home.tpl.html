AngularJS - Best Practice

What does this App?

It contains some examples of angular components and their best practice usages.

Why is this best practice?

There are some projects and persons out there, defining some pseudo standards using angular. I'll adapted their ideas and created this project to combine everything for me and others. Feel free to let me know if you got some better ideas. :)

Inspirations:
